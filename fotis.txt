Title                    Fortress of the Infernal Sanctum
Filename                 fotis.pk3
Advanced engine needed   GZDoom (Vulkan/GL renderer)
Primary purpose          Single Player
Author                   GOATPRST (Nate Roe)

Description              A single large map featuring defensive fortress
                         architecture, ~1052 enemies, an adaptive music
                         system with 18 segments, and a cinematic title
                         screen with a scale maquette diorama.

Additional Credits to    Ukiro for OTEX. I mean, like, wow.
                         Deep Star Maps for skybox imagery.

Special Thanks to        GZDoom and UDB devs, and documentation and wiki
                         contributors: Thanks for the engine, the tools,
                         and all the info!
